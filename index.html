The Candidate

A Reliable Choice

As a student here for the past five years, I've experienced the best of our community. I've also recognized where we can grow to meet the challenges of the modern world.

Running for Head Boy isn't about status for me. It's about service. I want to use this position to ensure that every student feels their concerns are met with action, not just words.

I am committed to transparency, empathy, and a practical approach to making our school the best it can be.

Commitments

Core Focus Areas

Empowered Council

Wellbeing Spaces

Introducing more 'quiet zones' and social hubs that prioritize mental health, allowing students to recharge during high-pressure exam periods.

Modern Innovation

Advocating for better digital integration in our learning spaces and sustainable campus practices that reflect our community's values.
